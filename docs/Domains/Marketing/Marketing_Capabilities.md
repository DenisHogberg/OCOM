# Marketing Capabilities

**Document ID:** DOMAIN-MARKETING-CAPABILITIES-01

**Status:** Draft

**Version:** 0.1

**Last Updated:** 21 July 2026

---

# Purpose

This document defines the Capabilities provided by the Marketing Domain.

Marketing Capabilities expose standardized business functionality for planning, executing, governing, and optimizing market communication while preserving Object Ownership, Lifecycle integrity, and enterprise interoperability.

Capabilities define what the Domain is able to perform rather than how those functions are implemented.

---

# Definition

A Marketing Capability is a business capability provided by the Marketing Domain.

Capabilities operate on Marketing Objects, coordinate marketing activities, and produce business outcomes consistent with applicable Policies and enterprise governance.

Capability semantics are defined by the Meta specification.

---

# Business Meaning

Marketing Capabilities enable organizations to consistently communicate commercial value, strengthen brand presence, engage target audiences, and execute marketing initiatives across markets and communication channels.

Capabilities expose reusable business functionality independent of implementation technologies.

---

# Design Principles

Marketing Capabilities shall:

- operate on Marketing Objects;
- preserve Object Ownership;
- respect Lifecycle definitions;
- comply with applicable Policies;
- support enterprise interoperability;
- remain technology independent.

---

# Capability Categories

Marketing Capabilities may be organized into the following categories.

## Campaign Management

Capabilities supporting Campaign execution.

Examples include:

- create Campaign;
- update Campaign;
- approve Campaign;
- launch Campaign;
- suspend Campaign;
- complete Campaign.

---

## Brand Management

Capabilities supporting Brand governance.

Examples include:

- create Brand;
- update Brand;
- publish Brand;
- manage Brand Assets;
- retire Brand.

---

## Audience Management

Capabilities supporting audience definition.

Examples include:

- define Audience;
- segment Audience;
- manage Personas;
- validate Audience;
- retire Audience.

---

## Content Management

Capabilities supporting marketing communication.

Examples include:

- create Content;
- review Content;
- publish Content;
- retire Content;
- manage Creative Assets.

---

## Channel Management

Capabilities supporting communication channels.

Examples include:

- register Channel;
- activate Channel;
- update Channel;
- suspend Channel;
- retire Channel.

---

## Promotion Management

Capabilities supporting Promotions.

Examples include:

- create Promotion;
- approve Promotion;
- launch Promotion;
- extend Promotion;
- complete Promotion.

---

## Planning Management

Capabilities supporting strategic planning.

Examples include:

- define Marketing Strategy;
- manage Marketing Calendar;
- allocate Budget;
- define Marketing Goals;
- manage Marketing Programs.

---

## Performance Management

Capabilities supporting continuous improvement.

Examples include:

- evaluate Campaign;
- analyze Channel performance;
- monitor KPIs;
- optimize marketing activities;
- generate performance reports.

---

## Marketing Governance

Capabilities supporting enterprise governance.

Examples include:

- enforce Marketing Policies;
- approve marketing activities;
- review compliance;
- manage approvals;
- govern marketing portfolio.

---

# Capability Consumers

Marketing Capabilities may be consumed by:

- Product;
- CRM;
- BI;
- Finance;
- Compliance;
- AI;
- other enterprise Domains.

Capability consumption does not transfer Object Ownership.

---

# Capability Governance

Marketing Capabilities shall be governed through:

- Ownership;
- Policies;
- Contracts;
- Constraints;
- version management.

Governance ensures consistent enterprise marketing behavior.

---

# Capability Evolution

Marketing Capabilities may evolve through:

- additional business functionality;
- refined marketing models;
- compatibility-preserving enhancements;
- expanded interoperability.

Breaking Capability changes shall require explicit governance.

---

# Relationship to Other Specifications

Marketing Capabilities build upon:

- Capability
- Object
- Lifecycle
- Process
- Event
- Policy
- Domain Governance

Additional Marketing specifications define the business context in which these Capabilities operate.

---

# Independence

The Marketing Capabilities specification does not prescribe:

- marketing automation platforms;
- campaign software;
- CRM systems;
- advertising technologies;
- implementation technologies.

Capabilities remain logical business functionality independent of implementation.

---

# Conformance

A conforming Marketing implementation shall:

- define standardized Marketing Capabilities;
- preserve Object Ownership;
- support governed Capability execution;
- maintain interoperability with other Domains;
- remain compatible with the OCOM Specification.
