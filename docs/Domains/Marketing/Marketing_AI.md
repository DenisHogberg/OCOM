# Marketing AI

**Document ID:** DOMAIN-MARKETING-AI-01

**Status:** Draft

**Version:** 0.1

**Last Updated:** 21 July 2026

---

# Purpose

This document defines how Artificial Intelligence (AI) participates within the Marketing Domain.

AI assists marketing activities by supporting planning, analysis, content generation, audience understanding, optimization, and decision preparation while preserving human governance, Object Ownership, and Lifecycle integrity.

AI participation remains advisory unless explicitly authorized by applicable Policies.

---

# Definition

Marketing AI represents the application of AI Capabilities to Marketing Objects and Marketing Processes.

AI operates on Marketing knowledge, Relationships, Events, Policies, and Lifecycles without assuming Ownership of Marketing Objects or governance responsibilities.

AI semantics are defined by the AI specifications of the OCOM framework.

---

# Business Meaning

AI enables organizations to improve the effectiveness, consistency, and efficiency of market communication while maintaining transparency, accountability, and enterprise governance.

AI augments marketing decisions but does not replace accountable business ownership.

---

# Design Principles

AI participation within the Marketing Domain shall:

- preserve Object Ownership;
- preserve Lifecycle integrity;
- operate within applicable Policies;
- produce traceable outcomes;
- support explainability;
- remain human-governed;
- remain technology independent.

# AI Decision Model

AI may:

- recommend;
- classify;
- summarize;
- analyze;
- predict;
- explain.

AI shall not inherently:

- approve Campaigns;
- publish marketing assets;
- redefine Marketing Policies;
- bypass governance;
- modify Object Ownership;
- replace accountable business decisions.

Business accountability remains outside AI.

---

# AI Inputs

AI may consume:

- Marketing Objects;
- Marketing Relationships;
- Marketing Events;
- Marketing Policies;
- Marketing Lifecycles;
- marketing documentation;
- enterprise knowledge.

Input availability depends upon applicable access controls.

---

# AI Outputs

AI may produce:

- recommendations;
- analyses;
- classifications;
- summaries;
- forecasts;
- risk indicators;
- draft marketing assets;
- supporting documentation.

AI outputs do not constitute authoritative Marketing data unless accepted through governed business processes.

---

# Human Governance

Organizations shall define governance determining:

- when AI may advise;
- when human approval is required;
- when AI-generated content may be accepted;
- how AI recommendations are reviewed;
- how accountability is maintained.

AI shall remain subject to enterprise governance.

---

# Explainability

Organizations should ensure AI-supported Marketing decisions remain explainable.

Explainability may include:

- reasoning summaries;
- supporting evidence;
- referenced Marketing Objects;
- applied Policies;
- confidence indicators.

Implementation mechanisms remain outside the scope of this specification.

---

# AI Governance

AI participation shall comply with:

- Marketing Policies;
- AI Policies;
- enterprise governance;
- applicable regulations;
- security requirements.

AI governance shall preserve trust, transparency, and accountability.

---

# AI Evolution

Marketing AI Capabilities may evolve through:

- improved analytical models;
- expanded marketing knowledge;
- enhanced recommendation quality;
- compatibility-preserving refinements.

AI evolution shall not modify Marketing semantics defined by this specification.

---

# Relationship to Other Specifications

Marketing AI builds upon:

- AI

Additional AI specifications define enterprise-wide AI behavior.

# Independence

This specification does not prescribe:

- foundation models;
- machine learning algorithms;
- marketing automation platforms;
- vector databases;
- inference platforms;
- implementation technologies.

Marketing AI remains implementation independent.

---

# Conformance

A conforming Marketing implementation shall:

- preserve Object Ownership;
- preserve Lifecycle integrity;
- ensure AI traceability;
- maintain human accountability;
- remain compatible with the OCOM Specification.
