# Financial Artificial Intelligence

**Document ID:** DOMAIN-FINANCE-AI-01

**Status:** Draft

**Version:** 0.1

**Last Updated:** 21 July 2026

---

# Purpose

This document defines the use of Artificial Intelligence (AI) within the Finance Domain.

AI capabilities assist financial operations by analyzing Financial Objects, interpreting financial context, supporting business decisions, and automating authorized activities while preserving Object Ownership, financial governance, and enterprise consistency.

---

# Definition

Financial AI represents AI capabilities operating within the Finance Domain.

AI assists financial activities but does not alter Domain responsibilities, Object Ownership, or governance.

AI behavior shall remain consistent with the AI specifications defined by the OCOM Specification.

---

# Business Meaning

AI enhances financial operations through intelligent analysis, forecasting, anomaly detection, reconciliation assistance, and decision support.

AI functions as a business capability rather than an autonomous financial authority.

Business accountability remains with the owning organization.

---

# Design Principles

Financial AI shall:

- operate on Financial Objects;
- preserve Object Identity;
- respect Domain Ownership;
- comply with applicable Policies;
- produce explainable outputs;
- remain technology independent.

---

# AI Scope

Financial AI may assist with:

- financial anomaly detection;
- reconciliation assistance;
- journal validation;
- financial forecasting;
- balance analysis;
- revenue and expense analysis;
- financial reporting support;
- operational decision support.

Organizations may define additional AI capabilities.

---

# AI Context

Financial AI may consume business context including:

- Financial Objects;
- Relationships;
- Events;
- Lifecycles;
- Policies;
- Contracts;
- enterprise Memory;
- historical financial activity.

Context availability is organization-specific.

---

# AI Outputs

Financial AI may produce:

- recommendations;
- classifications;
- predictions;
- summaries;
- explanations;
- suggested actions;
- confidence assessments.

AI outputs shall not become business facts until accepted through applicable governance.

---

# AI Decision Support

Financial AI may assist business decisions by:

- identifying financial anomalies;
- recommending accounting actions;
- supporting financial reconciliation;
- prioritizing financial investigations;
- forecasting financial outcomes.

Final business authority remains outside AI unless organizational Policies explicitly authorize automated execution.

---

# AI Governance

Financial AI shall operate under:

- Domain Governance;
- AI Governance;
- applicable Policies;
- applicable Contracts;
- applicable Constraints.

AI activities shall remain traceable and auditable.

---

# AI Memory

Financial AI may consume and contribute to enterprise Memory.

Memory usage shall comply with:

- Memory Governance;
- evidence requirements;
- retention policies;
- privacy requirements.

Memory Ownership remains unchanged.

---

# AI Collaboration

Financial AI may collaborate with AI capabilities operating in other Domains.

Collaboration shall occur through:

- Events;
- Capabilities;
- Contracts;
- governed business context.

Direct access to another Domain's internal implementation shall not be assumed.

---

# AI Evolution

Financial AI capabilities may evolve through:

- improved reasoning;
- enhanced analytical capabilities;
- expanded financial context;
- updated Policies;
- compatibility-preserving improvements.

Breaking behavioral changes shall require explicit governance.

---

# Relationship to Other Specifications

Financial AI builds upon:

- AI
- AI Governance
- Memory
- Capability
- Object
- Lifecycle
- Policy
- Contract
- Domain Governance

Additional Finance specifications define the business context in which AI operates.

---

# Independence

The Financial AI specification does not prescribe:

- AI models;
- machine learning algorithms;
- large language models;
- financial software;
- implementation technologies.

Organizations remain free to implement AI capabilities using technologies appropriate to their operational environment.

---

# Conformance

A conforming Finance implementation shall:

- define AI capabilities within the Finance Domain;
- preserve Object Ownership;
- comply with Domain Governance;
- support explainable and traceable AI behavior;
- remain compatible with the OCOM Specification.
