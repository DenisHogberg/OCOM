# Affiliate Policies

**Document ID:** DOMAIN-AFFILIATE-POLICIES-01

**Status:** Draft

**Version:** 0.1

**Last Updated:** 21 July 2026

---

# Purpose

This document defines the Policies governing the Affiliate Domain.

Affiliate Policies establish business rules for managing Partners, Referral Programs, Attribution, Commission Plans, Performance Profiles, and affiliate operations while preserving governance, Object Ownership, Lifecycle integrity, and enterprise interoperability.

Policies ensure affiliate partnerships operate consistently, transparently, fairly, and accountably.

---

# Definition

An Affiliate Policy is a business rule that constrains, authorizes, or guides behavior within the Affiliate Domain.

Policies govern Affiliate Objects, Processes, Capabilities, Relationships, and Events independently of implementation technologies.

Policy semantics are defined by the Core Policy specification.

---

# Business Meaning

Affiliate Policies define how enterprise affiliate partnerships shall be established, managed, evaluated, and governed.

They provide consistent business rules for partner onboarding, referral attribution, commercial collaboration, performance management, and operational governance.

Policies reduce operational, commercial, and compliance risks while ensuring fair and transparent partner relationships.

---

# Design Principles

Affiliate Policies shall:

- define explicit business rules;
- preserve Object Ownership;
- preserve Lifecycle integrity;
- support auditability;
- remain version controlled;
- remain technology independent.

---

# Policy Scope

Affiliate Policies may govern:

- Affiliate Objects;
- Lifecycles;
- Relationships;
- Processes;
- Capabilities;
- Events;
- AI-assisted Affiliate activities.

Policies may also govern interactions with other Domains.

---

# Policy Categories

Affiliate Policies may be organized into the following categories.

---

## Partner Policies

Policies governing affiliate partnerships.

Examples include:

- Partner eligibility;
- Partner onboarding;
- Partner verification;
- Partner activation;
- Partner suspension;
- Partner termination.

---

## Referral Policies

Policies governing Referral Programs.

Examples include:

- Referral eligibility;
- attribution rules;
- referral validation;
- duplicate referral handling;
- referral expiration.

Referral Policies define business attribution rather than technical tracking mechanisms.

---

## Commission Policies

Policies governing commercial collaboration.

Examples include:

- Commission eligibility;
- Commission approval;
- incentive eligibility;
- Partner tier assignment;
- Commission adjustment.

Payment execution remains governed by the Payments Domain.

---

## Attribution Policies

Policies governing attribution.

Examples include:

- attribution ownership;
- attribution validation;
- attribution correction;
- attribution reconciliation;
- attribution dispute resolution.

---

## Performance Policies

Policies governing partner evaluation.

Examples include:

- performance review frequency;
- traffic quality standards;
- Partner classification;
- Partner ranking;
- performance improvement requirements.

---

## Operational Policies

Policies governing affiliate operations.

Examples include:

- Affiliate Manager assignment;
- communication standards;
- portfolio management;
- review scheduling;
- operational documentation.

---

## Governance Policies

Policies governing enterprise affiliate management.

Examples include:

- documentation standards;
- audit requirements;
- policy compliance;
- governance reviews;
- exception management.

---

# Policy Application

Policies may apply to:

- individual Affiliate Objects;
- Object categories;
- Processes;
- Capabilities;
- Relationships;
- Events.

Policy applicability shall be explicitly defined.

---

# Policy Enforcement

Organizations shall establish mechanisms for enforcing Affiliate Policies.

Enforcement mechanisms are implementation-specific and outside the scope of this specification.

Policy enforcement may result in:

- Lifecycle transitions;
- Affiliate Events;
- approvals;
- Partner reviews;
- governance actions.

---

# Policy Governance

Affiliate Policies shall be governed through:

- Ownership;
- approval;
- publication;
- version management;
- periodic review.

Governance shall ensure enterprise-wide consistency.

---

# Policy Evolution

Affiliate Policies may evolve through:

- business improvements;
- governance refinements;
- partnership experience;
- compatibility-preserving enhancements.

Breaking Policy changes shall require explicit governance.

---

# Relationship to Other Specifications

Affiliate Policies build upon:

- Policy
- Domain Governance
- Capability
- Lifecycle
- Object
- Contract
- Constraint

Additional Affiliate specifications define where individual Policies apply.

---

# Independence

This specification does not prescribe:

- affiliate platforms;
- partner management software;
- rule engines;
- workflow engines;
- implementation technologies.

Policies remain logical business rules independent of implementation.

---

# Conformance

A conforming Affiliate implementation shall:

- define explicit Affiliate Policies;
- assign Policy Ownership;
- govern Policy lifecycles;
- support Policy traceability;
- remain compatible with the OCOM Specification.
