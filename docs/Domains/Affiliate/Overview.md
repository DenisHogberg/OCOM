# Affiliate Domain

**Document ID:** DOMAIN-AFFILIATE-README-01

**Status:** Draft

**Version:** 0.1

**Last Updated:** 21 July 2026

---

# Purpose

The Affiliate Domain owns enterprise affiliate partnerships, referral relationships, partner onboarding, commercial collaboration, and affiliate performance management.

The Domain defines how the enterprise establishes, governs, and evaluates relationships with external partners that generate business opportunities while preserving Object Ownership, Lifecycle integrity, and enterprise interoperability.

Affiliate enables organizations to manage partner ecosystems independently of Customers, Marketing activities, Products, and Payments.

---

# Definition

The Affiliate Domain is responsible for the planning, coordination, execution, and governance of affiliate partnerships.

It owns Affiliate Objects representing Partners, Affiliate Accounts, Referral Programs, Tracking Links, Traffic Sources, Commission Agreements, and Partner Performance.

Affiliate does not own Customers, Products, Payments, Contracts, or Financial Records.

---

# Business Meaning

Affiliate transforms external business relationships into governed commercial partnerships.

It provides the capabilities required to recruit, onboard, manage, evaluate, and optimize affiliates while maintaining accountability, transparency, and measurable business outcomes.

Affiliate serves as the enterprise domain responsible for partner-driven business acquisition.

---

# Domain Responsibilities

The Affiliate Domain is responsible for:

- affiliate onboarding;
- partner management;
- referral program management;
- commission program management;
- tracking management;
- traffic source management;
- partner performance management;
- affiliate relationship governance;
- affiliate communications.

---

# Owned Entities

Typical Affiliate Objects include:

- Affiliate;
- Affiliate Account;
- Partner;
- Referral Program;
- Commission Plan;
- Tracking Link;
- Tracking Identifier;
- Traffic Source;
- Referral Attribution;
- Partner Performance Profile.

Ownership of Affiliate Objects remains within the Affiliate Domain.

---

# Domain Boundaries

The Affiliate Domain owns affiliate partnerships and referral relationships.

It does not own:

- Customers;
- Products;
- Payments;
- Financial Records;
- Contracts;
- Compliance decisions.

These Objects remain owned by their respective Domains.

---

# Relationships with Other Domains

The Affiliate Domain collaborates with multiple enterprise Domains.

Typical interactions include:

**CRM**

Provides Customer identity and referral outcomes.

**Marketing**

Provides campaigns, creative assets, and promotional materials used by affiliates.

**Product**

Provides Products and commercial offerings promoted through affiliate channels.

**Payments**

Provides commission payment execution.

**Finance**

Provides accounting and financial reporting for affiliate commissions.

**Compliance**

Provides regulatory guidance, partner eligibility requirements, and policy validation.

**BI**

Provides performance analytics and business reporting.

**AI**

Provides intelligent assistance supporting affiliate optimization and partner analysis.

Ownership of Objects is preserved across all Domain interactions.

---

# Design Principles

The Affiliate Domain shall:

- preserve Object Ownership;
- preserve Lifecycle integrity;
- govern affiliate relationships;
- support enterprise interoperability;
- emit governed Events;
- remain technology independent.

---

# Domain Governance

The Affiliate Domain shall define governance for:

- Affiliate Objects;
- Affiliate Processes;
- Affiliate Capabilities;
- Affiliate Policies;
- Affiliate Events;
- Affiliate Lifecycles.

Governance shall ensure consistency, traceability, transparency, and accountability.

---

# Domain Evolution

The Affiliate Domain may evolve through:

- additional Affiliate Objects;
- expanded Capabilities;
- refined Processes;
- enhanced governance;
- compatibility-preserving improvements.

Evolution shall preserve interoperability with other OCOM Domains.

- affiliate software;
- partner management platforms;
- tracking systems;
- attribution platforms;
- implementation technologies.
