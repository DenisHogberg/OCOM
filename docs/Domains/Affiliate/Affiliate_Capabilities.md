# Affiliate Capabilities

**Document ID:** DOMAIN-AFFILIATE-CAPABILITIES-01

**Status:** Draft

**Version:** 0.1

**Last Updated:** 21 July 2026

---

# Purpose

This document defines the business Capabilities owned by the Affiliate Domain.

Affiliate Capabilities represent the business functions required to establish, manage, optimize, and govern affiliate partnerships while preserving Object Ownership, Lifecycle integrity, governance, and enterprise interoperability.

Capabilities define what the Affiliate Domain is able to perform rather than how those activities are implemented.

---

# Definition

An Affiliate Capability is a business function owned by the Affiliate Domain.

Capabilities operate on Affiliate Objects according to applicable Policies and Lifecycles while producing governed business outcomes.

Capability semantics are defined by the Core Capability specification.

---

# Business Meaning

Affiliate Capabilities enable organizations to build and operate sustainable partner ecosystems.

They provide standardized business functions for recruiting partners, managing referral programs, governing attribution, evaluating performance, and coordinating commercial relationships.

---

# Design Principles

Affiliate Capabilities shall:

- preserve Object Ownership;
- preserve Lifecycle integrity;
- operate according to Policies;
- produce governed Events;
- support enterprise interoperability;
- remain technology independent.

---

# Capability Categories

Affiliate Capabilities may be organized into the following categories.

---

## Partner Management

Capabilities supporting Partner operations.

Examples include:

- register Partner;
- verify Partner;
- approve Partner;
- activate Partner;
- suspend Partner;
- reinstate Partner;
- terminate Partner;
- manage Partner Profile.

---

## Referral Program Management

Capabilities supporting Referral Programs.

Examples include:

- create Referral Program;
- publish Referral Program;
- update Referral Program;
- activate Referral Program;
- suspend Referral Program;
- retire Referral Program.

---

## Attribution Management

Capabilities supporting referral attribution.

Examples include:

- assign Tracking Link;
- generate Referral Code;
- validate Attribution;
- reconcile Attribution;
- maintain Attribution Records.

Attribution Capabilities govern business attribution independently of implementation technologies.

---

## Commission Management

Capabilities supporting commercial collaboration.

Examples include:

- create Commission Plan;
- update Commission Plan;
- calculate Commission;
- approve Commission;
- manage Partner Tier;
- manage Incentive Program.

Commission payment execution remains outside the Affiliate Domain.

---

## Performance Management

Capabilities supporting affiliate evaluation.

Examples include:

- evaluate Partner Performance;
- assess Traffic Quality;
- calculate Partner Score;
- manage Performance Profile;
- classify Partner Tier.

---

## Relationship Management

Capabilities supporting partner collaboration.

Examples include:

- assign Affiliate Manager;
- manage Partner Communications;
- schedule Partner Reviews;
- coordinate Business Meetings;
- maintain Partner Portfolio.

---

## Governance Management

Capabilities supporting affiliate governance.

Examples include:

- validate Policy Compliance;
- coordinate Compliance Review;
- manage Operational Audits;
- enforce Governance Rules;
- maintain Business Documentation.

---

## Partnership Optimization

Capabilities supporting continuous improvement.

Examples include:

- identify Growth Opportunities;
- optimize Referral Programs;
- improve Partner Performance;
- optimize Commission Structures;
- evaluate Portfolio Performance.

---

# Capability Consumers

Affiliate Capabilities may be consumed by:

- Marketing;
- CRM;
- Product;
- Payments;
- Finance;
- Compliance;
- BI;
- AI;
- other enterprise Domains.

Capability consumption does not transfer Object Ownership.

---

# Capability Governance

Affiliate Capabilities shall be governed through:

- Ownership;
- Policies;
- Contracts;
- Constraints;
- version management.

Governance shall ensure consistent enterprise behavior.

---

# Capability Evolution

Affiliate Capabilities may evolve through:

- additional business functions;
- expanded interoperability;
- refined governance;
- compatibility-preserving enhancements.

Capability evolution shall preserve business semantics.

---

# Relationship to Other Specifications

Affiliate Capabilities build upon:

- Capability
- Object
- Lifecycle
- Process
- Event
- Policy
- Domain Governance

Additional Affiliate specifications define how individual Capabilities interact with Affiliate Objects.

---

# Independence

This specification does not prescribe:

- affiliate software;
- partner portals;
- tracking systems;
- workflow engines;
- implementation technologies.

Affiliate Capabilities remain implementation independent.

---

# Conformance

A conforming Affiliate implementation shall:

- define standardized Affiliate Capabilities;
- preserve Object Ownership;
- preserve Lifecycle integrity;
- govern Capability evolution;
- remain compatible with the OCOM Specification.
