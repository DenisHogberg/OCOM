# Affiliate AI

**Document ID:** DOMAIN-AFFILIATE-AI-01

**Status:** Draft

**Version:** 0.1

**Last Updated:** 21 July 2026

---

# Purpose

This document defines how Artificial Intelligence (AI) participates within the Affiliate Domain.

AI assists enterprise affiliate operations by improving partner management, referral analysis, attribution quality, performance optimization, and commercial decision preparation while preserving Object Ownership, Lifecycle integrity, and human governance.

AI participation remains advisory unless explicitly authorized by applicable Policies.

---

# Definition

Affiliate AI represents the application of AI Capabilities to Affiliate Objects, Affiliate Processes, Relationships, Events, Policies, and partner knowledge.

AI operates within enterprise governance and does not become the owner of Affiliate Objects or business decisions.

AI semantics are defined by the AI specifications of the OCOM framework.

---

# Business Meaning

AI enables organizations to build more effective affiliate ecosystems by assisting with partner evaluation, performance optimization, attribution analysis, fraud detection, and strategic partnership development.

AI augments affiliate decision-making while preserving accountability, transparency, and governance.

---

# Design Principles

AI participation within the Affiliate Domain shall:

- preserve Object Ownership;
- preserve Lifecycle integrity;
- operate within applicable Policies;
- produce traceable outcomes;
- support explainability;
- remain human-governed;
- remain technology independent.

---

# AI Capability Categories

AI may support the following Affiliate activities.

---

## Partner Intelligence

Examples include:

- Partner classification;
- Partner segmentation;
- Partner quality assessment;
- Partner ranking;
- Partner portfolio analysis.

---

## Referral Intelligence

Examples include:

- referral attribution analysis;
- referral quality assessment;
- Referral Program optimization;
- referral anomaly detection;
- referral trend analysis.

---

## Attribution Intelligence

Examples include:

- attribution validation;
- attribution reconciliation recommendations;
- attribution confidence estimation;
- attribution conflict analysis;
- attribution pattern discovery.

---

## Commission Intelligence

Examples include:

- Commission Plan recommendations;
- incentive optimization;
- Partner tier recommendations;
- commission impact analysis;
- commercial scenario evaluation.

AI recommendations do not authorize commission payments.

## Performance Intelligence

Examples include:

- performance forecasting;

- Partner growth analysis;

- portfolio benchmarking.

---

## Relationship Intelligence

Examples include:

- Partner communication summarization;
- relationship health assessment;
- engagement recommendations;
- partnership opportunity identification;
- strategic account analysis.

---

## Governance Assistance

Examples include:

- Policy validation;
- documentation review;
- compliance support;
- audit preparation;
- governance consistency analysis.

---

## Knowledge Assistance

Examples include:

- semantic search;
- partnership summarization;
- document generation;
- business knowledge discovery;
- operational guidance.

---

# AI Decision Model

AI may:

- recommend;
- classify;
- summarize;
- analyze;
- predict;
- explain.

AI shall not inherently:

- approve Partners;
- activate Referral Programs;
- authorize Commission Plans;
- modify Affiliate Policies;
- change Object Ownership;
- replace accountable business decisions.

Business accountability remains outside AI.

---

# AI Inputs

AI may consume:

- Affiliate Objects;
- Affiliate Relationships;
- Affiliate Events;
- Affiliate Policies;
- Affiliate Lifecycles;
- partner documentation;
- enterprise business knowledge.

Input availability depends upon applicable access controls.

---

# AI Outputs

AI may produce:

- recommendations;
- analyses;
- classifications;
- summaries;
- predictions;
- opportunity indicators;
- risk indicators;
- supporting documentation.

AI outputs do not constitute authoritative Affiliate data unless accepted through governed business processes.

---

# Human Governance

Organizations shall define governance determining:

- when AI may advise;
- when human approval is required;
- when AI recommendations may be accepted;
- how AI analyses are reviewed;
- how accountability is maintained.

AI shall remain subject to enterprise governance.

---

# Explainability

Organizations should ensure AI-supported Affiliate decisions remain explainable.

Explainability may include:

- reasoning summaries;
- supporting evidence;
- referenced Affiliate Objects;
- applied Policies;
- confidence indicators.

Implementation mechanisms remain outside the scope of this specification.

---

# AI Governance

AI participation shall comply with:

- Affiliate Policies;
- AI Policies;
- enterprise governance;
- applicable regulations;
- security requirements.

AI governance shall preserve trust, transparency, and accountability.

---

# AI Evolution

Affiliate AI Capabilities may evolve through:

- improved analytical models;
- expanded partnership knowledge;
- enhanced recommendation quality;
- compatibility-preserving refinements.

AI evolution shall not modify Affiliate semantics defined by this specification.

---

Affiliate AI builds upon:

Additional AI specifications define enterprise-wide AI behavior.

# Independence

This specification does not prescribe:

- foundation models;
- machine learning algorithms;
- affiliate platforms;
- recommendation engines;
- vector databases;
- implementation technologies.

Affiliate AI remains implementation independent.

---

# Conformance

A conforming Affiliate implementation shall:

- preserve Object Ownership;
- preserve Lifecycle integrity;
- ensure AI traceability;
- maintain human accountability;
- remain compatible with the OCOM Specification.
