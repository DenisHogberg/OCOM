# AI Domain

**Document ID:** DOMAIN-AI-README-01

**Status:** Draft

**Version:** 0.1

**Last Updated:** 21 July 2026

---

# Purpose

The AI Domain owns enterprise Artificial Intelligence execution, reasoning, decision support, and AI operational artifacts.

It provides standardized Entities that represent AI activities while preserving Entity Ownership, Lifecycle integrity, governance, explainability, and enterprise interoperability.

The AI Domain enables AI to participate in enterprise operations without becoming the authoritative owner of business information.

---

# Definition

The AI Domain governs Entities that represent AI execution, reasoning, recommendations, evaluations, and operational interactions.

AI consumes Entities from other Domains but does not own them.

Business authority remains with the originating Domain.

---

# Business Meaning

The AI Domain provides enterprise intelligence by observing Entities, analyzing Events, generating Recommendations, coordinating AI Agents, and supporting decision making.

AI enhances enterprise operations while preserving governance and human accountability.

---

# Owns

The AI Domain owns Entities including:

- AI Agent
- AI Assistant
- AI Session
- AI Task
- AI Workflow
- AI Recommendation
- AI Observation
- AI Insight
- AI Decision Proposal
- AI Evaluation
- AI Reasoning Trace
- Prompt Template
- Prompt Version
- Tool Invocation
- Tool Result
- Model Profile
- AI Capability Profile
- AI Feedback
- AI Conversation
- AI Context Package

Additional AI Objects may be introduced by future specifications.

---

# Does Not Own

The AI Domain does not own:

- Customers
- Employees
- Products
- Payments
- Financial Records
- Marketing Campaigns
- Support Cases
- Compliance Decisions
- Contracts
- Business Policies
- Operational Work Items

Those Objects remain owned by their respective Domains.

---

# Cross-Domain Relationships

The AI Domain may interact with:

- CRM
- Product
- Operations
- Finance
- Payments
- Compliance
- Legal
- Marketing
- Affiliate
- Support
- HR
- BI

AI analyzes Entities owned by these Domains without assuming ownership.

---

# Architectural Principles

The AI Domain follows these principles:

- AI assists but does not govern.
- AI recommends but does not own decisions.
- AI observes Entities without redefining them.
- AI preserves Object Ownership.
- AI remains explainable.
- AI operates under enterprise Policies.
- AI respects Lifecycle governance.
- AI remains implementation independent.

---

# Relationship to Other Specifications

The AI Domain builds upon:

- Core Object
- Core Event
- Core Relationship
- Core Lifecycle
- Core Capability
- Core Policy

Additional AI specifications define reasoning, memory, governance, and execution.

---

# Independence

This specification does not prescribe:

- foundation models;
- LLM providers;
- inference engines;
- vector databases;
- orchestration frameworks;
- implementation technologies.

The AI Domain defines business semantics rather than implementation architecture.

---

# Conformance

A conforming implementation shall:

- define standardized AI Entities;
- preserve Entity Ownership;
- preserve explainability;
- preserve governance;
- support enterprise interoperability;
- remain compatible with the OCOM Specification.
