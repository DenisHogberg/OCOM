# Terminology

**Document ID:** Core-03

**Status:** Draft

**Version:** 0.1

**Last Updated:** 20 July 2026

---

# Purpose

This document defines the normative terminology used throughout the specification.

All terms defined here are authoritative.

---

# Organization

A structured operational system consisting of domains, entities, workflows, and relationships.

---

# Entity

A uniquely identifiable operational object with defined attributes, ownership, lifecycle, and state.

---

# Domain

A logical boundary responsible for governing one or more entities.

---

# Workflow

A defined sequence of operational actions that transforms the state of one or more entities.

---

# State

A condition describing the current operational status of an entity.

---

# Lifecycle

The complete sequence of states an entity may pass through during its existence.

---

# Owner

The role, domain, or organizational unit accountable for an entity.

---

# Relationship

A defined operational connection between two or more entities.

---

# Event

A recorded occurrence representing a meaningful operational change.

---

# Attribute

A property describing an entity.

---

# Identifier

A unique value distinguishing one entity from all others.

---

# Model

A structured representation of an operational system using the concepts defined by this specification.

---

# Specification

The complete collection of normative documents defining the operational modeling framework.
